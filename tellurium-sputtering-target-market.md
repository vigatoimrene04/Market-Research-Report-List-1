<p><p>The Tellurium Sputtering Target market comprises two main types: rotary and non-rotatable targets. Rotary targets are designed to allow continuous rotation during the sputtering process, enhancing uniform material deposition and extending target life. In contrast, non-rotatable targets remain stationary, making them suitable for applications where rotational motion is unnecessary or impractical. This distinction impacts performance, efficiency, and application suitability in thin-film deposition processes in various industries, including electronics and solar energy.</p></p>
