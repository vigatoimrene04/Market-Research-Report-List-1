<p><ul><li>Rigid Frame Motor Grader</li><li>Articulated Frame Motor Grader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction motor grader market encompasses two primary types: rigid frame motor graders and articulated frame motor graders. Rigid frame motor graders feature a fixed structure, providing stability and efficiency for tasks like grading and leveling. They are ideal for flat terrains. In contrast, articulated frame motor graders have a flexible joint between the front and rear sections, enhancing maneuverability and allowing for better performance on rough or uneven surfaces. Each type serves specific applications in construction and road maintenance.</p></p>
