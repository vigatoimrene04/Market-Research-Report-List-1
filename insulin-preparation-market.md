<p><strong>The Insulin Preparation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fast-acting</li><li>Intermediate-acting</li><li>Long-acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulin preparation market comprises three main types: fast-acting, intermediate-acting, and long-acting insulins. Fast-acting insulins are designed for rapid glucose control, typically administered before meals to manage spikes in blood sugar. Intermediate-acting insulins offer a longer duration of action, often used to maintain basal glucose levels throughout the day. Long-acting insulins provide a steady release of insulin over an extended period, helping to stabilize blood sugar levels with minimal fluctuations, making them suitable for overall diabetes management.</p></p>
