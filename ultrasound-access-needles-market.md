<p><ul><li>EUS-FNA</li><li>EUS-FNB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrasound Access Needles market primarily includes two types: EUS-FNA (Endoscopic Ultrasound Fine Needle Aspiration) and EUS-FNB (Endoscopic Ultrasound Fine Needle Biopsy). EUS-FNA is utilized for obtaining fluid or tissue samples from various organs, allowing for cytological evaluation. In contrast, EUS-FNB provides larger tissue samples, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and histopathological assessment. Both types are essential for minimally invasive procedures in digestive and oncological medicine, aiding in the diagnosis and treatment of various conditions.</p></p>
