<p><strong>The Fundus Photography Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OCT</li><li>Slit Lamps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fundus photography camera market primarily includes two types: Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) systems and slit lamps. OCT provides high-resolution cross-sectional images of the retina, aiding in the diagnosis of vision-related diseases. Slit lamps, on the other hand, combine a microscope with a light source to examine the eye's anterior segment and also facilitate fundus photography in certain setups. Both technologies enhance diagnostic capabilities, promoting better patient outcomes in ophthalmology through detailed imaging.</p></p>
