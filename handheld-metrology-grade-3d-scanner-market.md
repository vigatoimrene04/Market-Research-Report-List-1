<p><strong>The Handheld Metrology Grade 3D Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser</li><li>Structure Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld metrology-grade 3D scanner market includes two primary types: laser-based and structured light scanners. Laser scanners utilize laser beams to capture precise measurements and create detailed 3D models, making them ideal for complex geometries and high accuracy. Structured light scanners project a series of light patterns onto objects, capturing their surface contours and details through the distortion of these patterns. Both types are used across industries for quality control, reverse engineering, and product development due to their precision and portability.</p></p>
