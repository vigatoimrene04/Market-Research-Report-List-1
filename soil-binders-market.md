<p><ul><li>Chemical Binders</li><li>Physical Binders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soil binders market is divided into two main types: chemical binders and physical binders. Chemical binders involve the use of synthetic or natural substances that react with soil particles, enhancing cohesion and stability, commonly used in construction and road maintenance. Physical binders, on the other hand, include materials like fibers or granules that improve soil structure and reduce erosion by mechanically binding soil particles together. Both types are essential for improving soil performance in various engineering and agricultural applications.</p></p>
