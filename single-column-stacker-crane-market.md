<p><p>The Single Column Stacker Crane market can be categorized into two main types based on the fork design: Plate Fork and Double Fork. Plate Fork stackers feature a single flat fork, ideal for lifting and moving plates or flat materials, enhancing stability and ease of handling in narrow aisles. In contrast, Double Fork stackers utilize two forks, allowing for the simultaneous handling of multiple items or a wider range of product shapes, thereby improving efficiency and versatility in storage and logistics operations.</p></p>
