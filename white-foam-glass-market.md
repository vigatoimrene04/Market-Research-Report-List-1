<p><strong>The White Foam Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The white foam glass market is divided into two main types: closed cell and open cell. Closed cell foam glass features a dense structure where individual cells are sealed, providing excellent insulation, moisture resistance, and compressive strength. It is ideal for applications in construction and refrigeration. In contrast, open cell foam glass has interconnected cells, offering higher permeability and lower density, making it suitable for sound absorption and lightweight applications. Each type serves distinct functions based on thermal and acoustic requirements.</p></p>
