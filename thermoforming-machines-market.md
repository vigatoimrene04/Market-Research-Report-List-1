<p><p>The thermoforming machines market is segmented into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic machines. Manual thermoforming machines require operator intervention for each step, making them suitable for small-scale production and prototyping. Semi-automatic machines offer a blend of automation and manual processes, enhancing efficiency while still requiring some operator involvement. Fully automatic thermoforming machines operate independently, streamlining production and minimizing labor costs, making them ideal for high-volume manufacturing and large-scale operations.</p></p>
