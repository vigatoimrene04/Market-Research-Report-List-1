<p><p>The Floating Storage and Offloading Unit (FSO) market encompasses various offshore units crucial for the oil and gas industry. FSOs are designed to store crude oil from production sites. Floating Production Storage and Offloading (FPSO) units not only store oil but also process it. Floating Drilling Production Storage and Offloading (FDPSO) combines drilling and production capabilities. Lastly, Floating Storage Regasification Units (FSRU) facilitate the conversion of liquefied natural gas back to its gaseous state for distribution, thus enhancing energy supply efficiency.</p></p>
