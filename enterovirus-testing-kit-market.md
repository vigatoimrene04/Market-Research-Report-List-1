<p><p>The Enterovirus Testing Kit market comprises two primary types: RT-PCR and Rapid Tests. RT-PCR (Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction) is a highly sensitive method used for the detection of enteroviruses through the amplification of viral RNA, making it suitable for laboratory settings. In contrast, Rapid Tests provide quicker results, often within hours, by detecting viral antigens or antibodies, and are useful in point-of-care settings. Both types play essential roles in diagnostics, guiding treatment decisions, and managing outbreaks.</p></p>
