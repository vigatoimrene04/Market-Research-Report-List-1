<p><p>The wearable AMOLED driver ICs market includes three primary package types: COG (Chip-On-Glass), COF (Chip-On-Flex), and COP (Chip-On-Paper). COG integrates the IC directly onto the display glass, enhancing durability and reducing size, ideal for slimmer devices. COF uses flexible substrates allowing for more versatile designs and easy integration in wearable technology. COP combines the IC with lightweight materials like paper, optimizing for ultra-thin applications while maintaining performance. Together, these packages cater to diverse wearable device needs.</p></p>
