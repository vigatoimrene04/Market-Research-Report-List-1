<p><strong>The Mechanical Gripping System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Gripping System</li><li>Centric Gripping System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical gripping system market is primarily categorized into two types: parallel gripping systems and centric gripping systems. Parallel gripping systems utilize opposing jaws that move in parallel to grasp objects, making them suitable for handling a variety of shapes and sizes. In contrast, centric gripping systems, which feature jaws that move toward a central axis, provide even force distribution and are ideal for gripping cylindrical or symmetrical objects. Both systems are crucial for automating manufacturing and assembly processes.</p></p>
