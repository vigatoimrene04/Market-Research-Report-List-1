<p><p>The air paint sprayer market is primarily divided into two types: pneumatic and manual sprayers. Pneumatic sprayers utilize compressed air to atomize paint, allowing for a fine, even application, ideal for large surfaces and professional projects. Manual sprayers, on the other hand, rely on hand-operated mechanisms to apply paint, offering greater control and precision for smaller tasks and detailed work. Both types cater to different user needs, with pneumatic sprayers suited for efficiency and speed, while manual sprayers emphasize precision and versatility.</p></p>
