<p><p>The non-vascular lumen guidewire market includes two primary types: straight guidewires and elbow guidewires. Straight guidewires are designed for direct access to specific anatomical sites, facilitating straightforward navigation through lumens for various medical procedures. In contrast, elbow guidewires feature a preformed bend, allowing for easier maneuverability in challenging anatomical configurations, particularly in tight curves or complex pathways. Both types are essential tools in minimally invasive interventions, ensuring precision and safety during diagnostic and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
