<p><p>The paving asphalt market encompasses various types catering to specific applications. Mix Type asphalt combines aggregates and binder for versatile uses. Perpetual Type features layers designed for long-lasting performance, ideal for heavy traffic. Porous Type allows water drainage to reduce hydroplaning, enhancing safety. Quiet Type minimizes noise from traffic, improving urban environments. Warm-Mix Type is produced at lower temperatures, reducing energy consumption and emissions, making it an environmentally friendly option. Each type addresses unique performance and environmental needs in paving.</p></p>
