<p><p>The ceramic urinals market encompasses two primary types: waterless and flush type. In contrast, flush type urinals rely on a water supply to rinse waste, ensuring cleanliness through regular flushing. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences and environmental considerations, offering options for public restrooms, commercial spaces, and residential installations.</p></p>
