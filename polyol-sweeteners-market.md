<p><p>Polyol sweeteners, also known as sugar alcohols, are low-calorie sweeteners derived from natural sources. Commonly used in food and beverage products, they provide sweetness with fewer calories and lower glycemic responses compared to regular sugar. Notably popular in sugar-free and reduced-calorie products, polyols include mannitol, sorbitol, xylitol, and erythritol. Their unique properties make them suitable for a variety of applications ranging from confectioneries to baked goods.</p><p>The Polyol Sweeteners Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.00% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by rising health awareness among consumers and increased demand for low-sugar, low-calorie alternatives. Additionally, the trend towards clean label products is enhancing the appeal of polyol sweeteners, as they are often derived from natural sources and do not contain artificial ingredients. The growing popularity of ketogenic and low-carb diets further fuels the demand for these sweeteners, as they fit well within these dietary preferences. Innovations in product formulations, coupled with an expanding range of applications across the food and pharmaceutical industries, are also contributing to the market's expansion, positioning polyol sweeteners as a favorable choice for health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
