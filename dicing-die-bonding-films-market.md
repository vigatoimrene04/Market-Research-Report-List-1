<p><p>The dicing die bonding films market includes two primary types: UV curing type and normal type. UV curing films utilize ultraviolet light to initiate a rapid curing process, providing quick adhesion and excellent precision, making them suitable for high-speed production. Normal type films, on the other hand, cure through traditional heat or chemical processes, offering versatility but typically longer curing times. Both types serve crucial roles in semiconductor packaging, ensuring reliable die attachment in various applications.</p></p>
