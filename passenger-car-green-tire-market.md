Radial green tires feature a construction where the internal ply cord is arranged radially, enhancing fuel efficiency, durability, and performance on the road. In contrast, bias green tires have layers of cord arranged diagonally, providing increased stability and toughness, often preferred for specific applications like off-road driving. Both types cater to different consumer needs based on vehicle performance and usage conditions.</p></p>
