<p><p>Microplate readers are essential laboratory instruments used to detect biological, chemical, or physical events in microplates. Optical filter microplate readers utilize specific wavelength filters to select light before detecting signals, ensuring high sensitivity and specificity for various assays. In contrast, optical grating microplate readers rely on diffraction gratings to disperse light, allowing for the simultaneous measurement of multiple wavelengths, enhancing versatility and enabling complex assays. Together, these technologies cater to diverse research needs in fields like drug discovery and diagnostics.</p></p>
