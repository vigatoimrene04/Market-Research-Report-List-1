<p><p>Actuated butterfly valves are control devices used to regulate the flow of liquids and gases in various industrial applications. <p><p>The actuated butterfly valves market encompasses various types based on their actuation mechanisms. Electrically actuated butterfly valves use electric motors to control valve movement, offering precise control and responsiveness, making them suitable for applications requiring automation. Pneumatically actuated butterfly valves, on the other hand, utilize compressed air for actuation, delivering quick response times and robust performance in high-pressure settings. Both types are essential in managing fluid flow in industries such as water treatment, oil and gas, and manufacturing.</p></p>
