<p><p>The electric control valve market is categorized into several types based on flow paths. </p><p>**Single Way valves** allow flow in one direction, used for simple on-off control. </p><p>**Two Way valves** regulate flow in two directions, commonly found in industrial processes for controlling fluid movement effectively.</p><p>**Three Way valves** enable diversions or mixing of fluids, essential for applications requiring complex flow management.</p><p>**Other** types include specialized valves with unique configurations catering to specific industrial needs, enhancing system efficiency and adaptability.</p></p>
