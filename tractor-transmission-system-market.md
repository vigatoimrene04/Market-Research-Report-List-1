<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tractor transmission system market can be classified into two primary types: mechanical and hydraulic. Mechanical transmission systems utilize gears and clutches to transfer power from the engine to the wheels, offering efficiency and direct control. In contrast, hydraulic transmission systems use hydraulic fluid to transmit power, allowing for smoother operation and better torque management. Each type caters to different agricultural needs, with mechanical being favored for simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while hydraulic systems provide advanced performance and ease of use in challenging conditions.</p></p>
