<p><p>Cyclone separation is a technique used to separate particles from a gas or liquid mixture using centrifugal force. This process involves the spinning of the mixture within a cyclone chamber, allowing heavier particles to be expelled outwards and collected at the bottom, while lighter particles exit through the top. Cyclone separators are widely utilized in various industries, including mining, food processing, and waste management, due to their efficiency and low operating costs.</p><p>The Cyclone Separation Market is anticipated to grow significantly, driven by increasing industrialization and the rising demand for efficient air and liquid filtration systems.
