<p><p>The sabre saw blades market primarily features two types: T-Shank and U-Shank blades. T-Shank blades have a tang that fits securely into the saw, ensuring stability and easy blade changes. They are popular for precision cuts in various materials. U-Shank blades, with a simple U-shaped slot, are typically found in older saw models and are less common in modern power tools. Both types serve distinct purposes, catering to different user needs and equipment compatibility in the woodworking and construction sectors.</p></p>
