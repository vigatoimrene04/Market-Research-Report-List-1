<p><p>The passive buzzer market can be categorized into two main types: stationary and pogo pin buzzers. Stationary buzzers are fixed in place, designed for applications where a stable sound output is required. In contrast, pogo pin buzzers come equipped with spring-loaded pins that allow for temporary connections, making them ideal for portable devices or applications requiring quick assembly. Both types serve distinct functions, from alarms to notification systems, offering versatility in sound signaling across various industries.</p></p>
