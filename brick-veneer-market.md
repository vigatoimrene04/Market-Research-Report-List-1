<p><p>The brick veneer market consists of various types, including adhered veneer and anchored veneer. Adhered veneer is directly attached to the substrate using adhesives, offering a lighter option for construction. Anchored veneer, on the other hand, is secured with metal anchors, providing greater stability and structural support, ideal for taller buildings.
