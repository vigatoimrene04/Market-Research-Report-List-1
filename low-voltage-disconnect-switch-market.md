<p><strong>The Low Voltage Disconnect Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fused</li><li>Non-Fused</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Voltage Disconnect Switch market is divided into two main types: fused and non-fused switches. Fused disconnect switches incorporate a fuse, providing protection against overloads and short circuits by interrupting the circuit when excessive current flows. This enhances safety and equipment longevity. Non-fused disconnect switches, on the other hand, do not have a built-in fuse; they rely on external protective devices. They are often more compact and suitable for applications where overload protection isn't critical, allowing for more design flexibility.</p></p>
