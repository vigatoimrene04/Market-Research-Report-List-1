Optically variable inks exhibit color changes when viewed from different angles, popular in currency and high-security documents. Humidity sensitive inks respond to moisture, indicating exposure. Infrared fluorescent inks are used for hidden security features. Pressure sensitive inks change with applied pressure, making them useful in packaging and labels.
