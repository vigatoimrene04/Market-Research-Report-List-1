<p><p>The vermiculite mining market is categorized into two main types: crude vermiculite and exfoliated vermiculite. Crude vermiculite is the raw form, extracted directly from mines and used primarily in industrial applications. Exfoliated vermiculite, on the other hand, is processed by heating the crude material, expanding its volume significantly. This expanded form is lighter and is commonly utilized in construction, horticulture, and insulation, due to its excellent thermal properties and moisture retention capabilities.</p></p>
