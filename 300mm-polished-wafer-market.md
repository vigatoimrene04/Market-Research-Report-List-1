<p><ul><li>P Type</li><li>N Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 300mm polished wafer market is categorized into two primary types: P-type and N-type wafers. P-type wafers are doped with acceptor impurities, creating a positive charge carrier, which enhances hole mobility. In contrast, N-type wafers are doped with donor impurities, generating negative charge carriers and boosting electron mobility. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, impacting device performance and efficiency. The choice between P-type and N-type generally depends on the specific application and desired electronic properties.</p></p>
